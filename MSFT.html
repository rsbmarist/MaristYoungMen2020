MSFT

Microsoft is a multinational technology company headquartered in Redmond, WA. It was founded by Bill Gates and Paull Allen in 1975. Microsoft was initially famous for developing the Windows Operating System which is used on almost 89% of desktops workstations, and laptops. However, as time has progressed Microsoft expanded its business to include Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Powerpoint), laptops, database servers, and cloud computing. Microsoft is currently traded on the NASDAQ Stock Exchange.
