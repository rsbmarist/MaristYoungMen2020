Hewlett-Packard is a multinational technology company headquartered in Palo Alto, CA. HP was founded in 1939 by David Packard, Bill Hewlett, and Mohamed M. Atalia. HP was initially famous for making printers. However, as time has progressed HP has expanded its product line to include laptops, workstations, and tablets. HP is currently traded on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE).
