CSCO

Cisco Systems is a multinational technology conglomerate headquartered in San Jose, CA. Cisco was founded in 1984 by Sandy Lerner and Leonard Bosack. Cisco is famous for selling networking devices such as routers and switches. However, as time has progressed Cisco has expanded its product line to include IoT devices, cloud computing, and security products. Cisco is currently traded on the NASDAQ Stock Exchange.
